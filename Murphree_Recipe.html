Mulled Cider

by Mark Murphree

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts apple cider
  • 1 tsp. whole allspice
  • 6 or more cloves
  • 1 sick cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/3 cup lemon juice

Directions

Simmer cider with spices and sugar for 30 minutes. Add juices and strain. Serve hot.

Makes 20 servings.
